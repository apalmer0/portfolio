One Portfolio to Rule Them All

Tired of his current, inadequate portfolio site and in need of a better way to present himself in light of some recent acclaim his work had received, Keith came to me to build him a better portfolio highlighting his accomplishments.

Though certainly nothing overly complicated or involved, this project was an incredible opportunity to take on a paying client and deliver a product that met or exceeded his needs. This ability to use my skills to produce something of value for another person is exactly why I want to be a developer in the first place.
