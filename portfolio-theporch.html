The Porch

Climbing. Community. Progress.

The Porch is a web app built for the indoor rock climbing community and climbing gym management. By connecting these two parties, The Porch endeavors to become a tool to further training, feedback, and community within the indoor rock climbing world.

I am by no means solely responsible for this project. I have had significant help from a number of developers worldwide, as well as numerous project managers who have contributed time and advice towards improving the product to become the valuable tool we know it can become. It is a labor of love that I consistently motivates me to learn and do more, every day.

  • Technologies: Angular, Ruby on Rails, MySQL
